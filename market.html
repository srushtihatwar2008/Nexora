💡 What Do These Numbers Mean?
Price per Unit (₹)

What the mandi is offering for your crop today

Distance (km)

How far the mandi is from you. Closer mandis = less spoilage, lower cost

Transport Time

How long to reach the mandi. Shorter time = fresher produce = better price

Transport Cost (₹)

Truck/vehicle cost to reach this mandi

Net Profit

This is what matters! = Price - Transport Cost - Spoilage Loss

📍 Why Does Distance Matter So Much?

Imagine two mandis offering the same base price:

❌ Mandi 100 km Away

Transport: ₹1500
Spoilage: 15%
Final: Low profit

✅ Mandi 10 km Away

Transport: ₹150
Spoilage: 2%
Final: High profit

💡 Closer mandi = Less cost + Fresher produce (less spoilage) = More money in your pocket

📐 Real Calculation: Tomato Example
Mandi A Mandi B
Base Price: ₹2500/10kg ₹2500/10kg
